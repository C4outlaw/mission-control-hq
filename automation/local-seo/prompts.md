## 1) GBP Category Audit
"Open Chrome and go to Google Maps. Search '[service] in [city]' for these 3 keywords: [keyword1], [keyword2], [keyword3]. For each search, note which competitors show in the Map Pack. Open each competitor GBP and extract primary category and all secondary categories. Put everything in a spreadsheet with one tab per keyword. Columns: business name, primary category, secondary categories, star rating, review count, ranking position. Highlight categories competitors have that my GBP is missing."

## 2) GBP Attributes Audit
"Open Chrome and compare my GBP [my_gbp_url] with competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. Extract all visible attributes/tags. Output spreadsheet columns: attribute, mine (yes/no), competitor1, competitor2, competitor3. Highlight attributes I’m missing. Also list attributes shared by all top competitors as baseline requirements."

## 3) Competitor Review Teardown
"Open competitor GBP URLs [url1], [url2], [url3]. Analyze last 50 reviews each. Extract total count, average rating, review velocity for 30/60/90 days, common service mentions, neighborhood mentions, recurring complaints. Compare against my GBP [my_gbp_url]. Output spreadsheet + tab showing exact monthly review target needed to catch top competitor and estimated timeline."

## 4) Review Response Strategy
"Compare review response behavior across my GBP [my_gbp_url] and competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. For last 30 responses each: response rate, speed, tone, service/location keyword usage, handling of negative reviews. Output comparison sheet and write template system with 3 variations each for 5-star, 4-star, 3-star, 1-2-star responses. Keep language human and non-robotic."

## 5) GBP Posts Strategy
"Audit GBP posts for my listing [my_gbp_url] and competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. Extract posting frequency in last 90 days, post types, image/CTA usage, topics. Build an 8-week posting calendar with 2–3 posts/week covering seasonal offers, project showcases, neighborhood mentions ([area1], [area2], [area3]), review highlights, team spotlights. Include target keywords: [keyword1], [keyword2], [keyword3]. Write full copy for first 4 weeks plus image suggestions."

## 6) Services Section Optimization
"Audit services section for my GBP [my_gbp_url] vs competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. Extract all listed services, description depth, groupings. Compare against my website [site_url] and flag services on website missing in GBP. Write optimized 2–3 sentence service descriptions for all core services [service1], [service2], [service3], naturally including target keywords and service areas [area1], [area2], [area3]."

## 7) GBP Description Optimization
"Extract GBP descriptions from my listing [my_gbp_url] and competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. Output spreadsheet with character count, keywords used, areas mentioned, USP, CTA presence. Identify competitive messaging gaps. Write 3 GBP description variants (max 750 chars): (1) keyword-focused, (2) conversion-focused, (3) balanced. Include keywords [keyword1], [keyword2], [keyword3] and areas [area1], [area2], [area3]."

## 8) GBP Photo Audit
"Compare photo strategy on my GBP [my_gbp_url] vs competitors [url1], [url2], [url3]. Count total photos, photos in last 90 days, photo types (team, jobs, before-after, office, trucks, equipment), likely stock indicators. Output spreadsheet and create 8-week upload plan with exact weekly counts and shot list focused on before-after, team in field, trucks in service areas, completed installs."
